## 🎮 Games

### Strand Commander
> *A Centipede-inspired arcade game with a hairstylist twist.*

**Play it:** Open `strand-commander/index.html` in any browser.

**How to Play:**
- `← →` or `A D` — Move your Comb Blaster
- `SPACE` or `↑` — Fire toner serum shots
- Destroy the Bad Hair Strand before it reaches you!

**Enemies:**
| Enemy | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Bad Hair Strand (segment) | 10 × level | The centipede — braided hair gone rogue |
| Tangle Spider | 300 | Crawls across the player zone |
| Split End Flea | 200 | Drops product bottles as it falls |
| Bleach Scorpion | 1000 | Poisons your product bottles |

**Brand Colors:**
- Hot Pink `#FA5185` — primary accent
- Cream `#F8EEE5` — background warmth
- Dark Green `#015A42` — depth accent
- Charcoal `#171411` — grounding dark
