## Concept 1 — Salon CEO ✅ APPROVED FOR BUILD
**Blog:** Hairstylist Burnout Is Real: 10 Salon Systems That Saved My Business
**URL:** https://mirellamanelli.com/hairstylist-burnout-salon-systems/
**Genre:** Time-management / triage simulation
**Gameplay:** The player runs a growing salon where chaos mounts in real time — empty booking system, walk-in clients, scrawled pricing, double-booked schedule. Each round the player must drag-and-drop the correct "system card" (automated booking, written contract, weekly CEO day, simplified menu, etc.) onto the right problem before a Burnout Meter fills. Getting the right system match freezes the problem and earns revenue; a wrong match or missed window costs energy. The game ends when the salon burns out or the player successfully implements all 10 systems and unlocks Salon CEO status.

## Concept 2 — Color Rescue
**Blog:** What Is Hair Color Correction? A Stylist's Guide to Fixing Hair Color Mistakes
**URL:** https://mirellamanelli.com/what-is-hair-color-correction/
**Genre:** Multi-stage puzzle / damage control
**Gameplay:** Players receive a client whose hair has been wrecked by a box dye disaster or bad DIY bleach. Complete a consultation phase, then work through 5 timed stages: consult, strand test, lift/remove, precision placement, and tone. Each stage presents a mini-puzzle and wrong choices cost "hair health points." Goal: deliver a corrected, healthy result before health points hit zero.

## Concept 3 — Lift Lab
**Blog:** BlondMe Bleach Lightener Review: Can It Really Lift Dark Hair?
**URL:** https://mirellamanelli.com/blondme-bleach-lightener-review/
**Genre:** Precision mixing / reaction runner
**Gameplay:** Select bleach formula strength and developer volume, then watch a real-time processing bar race across a color scale (1=jet black to 10=platinum). Stop the bar inside the target lift zone — too early = orange, too late = broken. Foil temperature and hair thickness add variability. Correct lifts bank gold and unlock bond protectors.

## Concept 4 — Gray Daze
**Blog:** Best Hair Color for Blending Gray Hair: Redken Shades EQ vs. Schwarzkopf IGORA Vibrance
**URL:** https://mirellamanelli.com/best-hair-color-for-blending-gray-hair/
**Genre:** Head-to-head matching / formula duel
**Gameplay:** Two clients sit side by side — one 30% gray, one 90% gray. Assign the right demi-permanent formula (Shades EQ or IGORA Vibrance), developer volume, and shade before the clock expires. Incorrect assignments cause banding, muddy tone, or early fade. Higher rounds add resistant gray and mixed-gray patterns.

## Concept 5 — Foil Frenzy
**Blog:** 8 Foil Highlight Techniques You Need to Know as a Hairstylist
**URL:** https://mirellamanelli.com/foil-highlight-techniques/
**Genre:** Pattern-matching rhythm game
**Gameplay:** A client's head scrolls toward the player and you must tap/drag foil sections in the correct pattern for each of the 8 techniques (slice, chunky, babylights, microlights, etc.) before the client reaches the end of the chair. Combo chains unlock the "Manelli Method" bonus round — the boss-tier pattern with the highest score multiplier.
